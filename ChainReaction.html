Chain Reaction - How to Play

Select number of teams playing. Set each team's name or use default given.

Teams need to complete the word chain by guessing words in it. On your turn, you are asked to unreveal a letter of the adjacent word to first or last word.

On successful guess of word, the team continue guessing the other words in the chain.

On wrong guess, next team need to unreveal a letter first and then guess the word.

Each correct guess = 10 points

Last word of the chain = 20 points
